Password types
Simple Passwords

Simple passwords are two random words, put together, followed by a number. They do not contain symbols. They're always kid-friendly!

Moderate Passwords

Moderate passwords are slightly more secure. They contain two random words put together, but will also include symbols and 3 numbers at the end.

Strong Passwords

Strong passwords are the most secure. They will contain a random mix of letters, numbers, and symbols. With strong passwords, several additional settings will show, allowing you more control of your generated passwords.
